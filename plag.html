Methodology

Four layers of detection

🔢
N-gram Fingerprinting

Slides a window of N consecutive words across both documents, hashing each sequence into a fingerprint. Shared fingerprints reveal copied passages even when punctuation changes.

📐
TF-IDF Cosine Similarity

Converts each document into a weighted term-frequency vector — rare words count more than common ones. The cosine angle between vectors gives an overall similarity score immune to length differences.

🧠
Semantic Paraphrase Detection

Sentence-by-sentence comparison using word-overlap and Jaccard similarity to catch paraphrased content where words change but meaning is preserved.

🔍
Structural Pattern Analysis

Analyses argument flow, sentence structure patterns and discourse markers to identify structural plagiarism — when an author mirrors the organization of another text.

🧹
Stopword Removal

Common words (the, a, and, is…) are stripped before analysis, so the comparison focuses on meaningful content words. Reduces false positives from shared boilerplate.

🔒
100% Local & Private

All analysis runs in your browser using pure JavaScript. Your documents never leave your device. No account, no storage, no tracking.
